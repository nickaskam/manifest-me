Funding Scenarios

4 tiers
Tier 0 — Bootstrapped $0 raised

Default Ramen Mode

Run lean, prove the concept, get to 100 paying users before spending a dollar on growth.

Team

  • Nick — product, engineering, marketing + ops (full time)
  • Paul — sales outreach, B2B partnerships (significant time)
  • Sandrine — brand, influencer network (part time / sweat)

Strategy

  • Fix min-instances=0 + migrate to Veo Lite → get infra cost to near $0/month
  • App Store launch — open to public, remove invite-only gate
  • Nick runs App Store listing, ASO, paid app install campaigns
  • Paul builds early sales pipeline — B2B wellness outreach, partner deals
  • Sandrine leverages creator network for organic content + influencer seeding
  • Goal: 100 paying subscribers within 60 days of launch
  • Use those 100 users as case studies + social proof for a raise

Milestone to unlock Tier 1

  • 100 paying subscribers + measurable retention (users coming back month 2)
Tier 1 — Friends & Family $50K raised

First Dollars — Prove Distribution

Small raise from personal network. No institutional investors yet. Use it entirely for user acquisition — not salaries.

Team

  • Nick — CEO, full time — owns marketing + engineering
  • Paul — CRO, significant time (sales outreach + B2B partnerships)
  • Sandrine — CMO, part time (brand, creator partnerships)

Strategy

  • Meta app install campaigns targeting law of attraction, manifestation, vision board audiences
  • TikTok organic content showing real video outputs — this category goes viral easily
  • Influencer seeding: gift free months to 10–20 mid-tier creators in the spiritual wellness space
  • Apply to YC, Techstars, or a wellness-focused accelerator — 100 paying users + a raise = a much stronger application

Equity given up

  • Friends & family: typically 5–10% on a SAFE note at $1.5–2M cap
  • Dilution is manageable at this stage — don't give up board seats

Milestone to unlock Tier 2

  • 500 paying subscribers + measurable CAC (cost to acquire a user)
Tier 2 — Pre-Seed Angel $150K raised

First Institutional Money — Scale What Works

Angel round or small syndicate. By this point you know which channel works. Double down on it.

Team

  • Nick — CEO, full time salary (~$4–5K/month)
  • Sandrine — CMO, full time salary (~$4–5K/month)
  • Paul — CRO, part-to-full time (~$3K/month)
  • 1 contract iOS/backend engineer ($5–7K/month, part time) — frees Nick to focus on CEO work

Tier 3 — Seed Round $250K raised

Seed Stage — Build the Machine

Institutional seed or larger angel syndicate. You've proven the model. Now systematize growth and build toward Series A.

Team

  • Nick — CEO, full time market salary (~$6–8K/month)
  • Sandrine — CMO, full time (~$6K/month)
  • Paul — CRO, full time (~$5–6K/month)
  • First full-time hire: Senior iOS + Backend Engineer ($8–10K/month) — Nick moves to full CEO role
  • Part-time: growth marketer or paid UA specialist

Strategy

  • Launch annual subscription tier (2 months free) — dramatically improves cash flow and LTV

  • Build B2B channel — Paul sells to corporate wellness, yoga studios, life coaching platforms

  • May trigger a board seat for lead investor — negotiate carefully
  • Option pool refresh likely required (expand to 15–18% for hiring)

Key Hires

Contractors first
Hire 1 — Immediate

UI/UX Designer

Biggest current skill gap on the team. Contract engagement to start — scoped project, not open-ended retainer.

  • Structure: Contract — per project or hourly, not a co-founder
  • Scope: App redesign, onboarding flow polish, paywall UI, App Store screenshots
  • Rate: $75–150/hr or $3–8K for a defined deliverable scope
  • Equity: None to start — revisit after seeing quality of work and fit
  • If they want equity later and it's earned: 0.5–1.5% from the option pool, 4-year vest
  • Do not offer founder equity — this role is scoped, not existential to the company direction
Hire 2 — At $150K+ Raise

iOS / Backend Engineer

Frees Nick to operate as CEO rather than staying heads-down in code. Only justifiable once there's funding to pay a real rate.

  • Structure: Contract part-time to start ($5–7K/month), convert to full-time at seed
  • Scope: Ship backlog features, backend tests, CI/CD setup — things Nick currently owns alone
  • Rate: $80–120/hr contract; $120–150K salary if full-time at seed stage
  • Equity: 0.5–1% from option pool if full-time; none for pure contract
